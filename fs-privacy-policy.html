Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

Welcome to FitSphere. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Offline-First Privacy

FitSphere is designed as an offline-first application. This means that nearly all data you enter—including your water intake, habits, workout goals, and calendar notes—is stored locally on your device only. We do not have servers that store your personal health data.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number. Any physical details entered (like weight or height used for calculators) are stored locally and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Local Storage (Hive)

The app uses a local database (Hive) to save your progress. This data stays on your phone. If you delete the app, this local data is typically deleted as well, depending on your device settings.

4. Permissions

FitSphere may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you reminders for water, habits, or calendar tasks.
  • Alarms: To ensure reminders trigger accurately even when the app is in the background.

5. Third-Party Services

Currently, FitSphere does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud sync services. Should this change in the future, this policy will be updated, and you will be notified.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the app.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact the developer directly through the support channel provided on the Google Play Store.
